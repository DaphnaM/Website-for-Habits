Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new habit?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen to create a new habit. You can customize the name, emoji, color, and set your preferred frequency and reminder times.

How do I set up reminders?

When creating or editing a habit, tap on "Reminder" to set a specific time. Make sure notifications are enabled for HabitApp in your device settings.

What is the NFC feature?

You can pair NFC tags with your habits for quick logging. Simply tap your phone on a paired NFC tag to mark a habit as complete. Great for habits tied to physical locations like your gym or desk.

How do I share a habit with someone?

Open the habit you want to share, tap the share icon, and generate a share code. Send this code to a friend or family member so they can add the same habit to their app.

Is my data synced across devices?

Yes! When you're signed in, your habits and progress sync automatically across all your devices.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at support@habitapp.com. We will process your request within 30 days.
